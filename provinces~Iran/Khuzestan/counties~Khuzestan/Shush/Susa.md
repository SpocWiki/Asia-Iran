# [[Susa]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[../../../../../../../../../WikiData/WD~Susa,180773|WD~Susa,180773]]  

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Susa** ( SOO-sə) was an ancient city in the lower Zagros Mountains 
> about 250 km (160 mi) east of the Tigris, between the Karkheh and Dez Rivers in Iran. 
> 
> One of the most important cities of the Ancient Near East, 
> Susa served as the capital of Elam and the winter capital of the Achaemenid Empire, 
> and remained a strategic centre during the Parthian and Sasanian periods.
>
> The site currently consists of three archaeological mounds, 
> covering an area of around 1 square kilometre (0.39 sq mi). 
> 
> The city of Shush is located on the site of ancient Susa.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susa)
